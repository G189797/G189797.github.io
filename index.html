Who am I ?

I am Graphic Designer based in Miami, Florida. I am student at Miami's International University of Art and Design. My major is Graphic and Web and I have experience  with coding on HTML and CSS I find it very beneficial to have experience in coding and web developing since most of my work is online.

What I do ?

I work on many different designs such as logos, posters, designing cover book pages, web design and I do a lot of illustrations. Illustrator is one of my main strengths where I have done most of my work on. I also have a passion for tracing using the pen-tool on illustrator where I have traced many different characters and turned them into something graphic.
